About Noemi Hernandez & La Familia Jumpers

Noemi Hernandez, the proud owner of La Familia Jumpers, embarked on her entrepreneurial journey driven by a childhood dream. Growing up, she witnessed her mother's struggles, waking up early to sell shoes at the street swap meet. These experiences instilled in her a determination to create a better life for herself and her family. Noemi's dream materialized when she purchased two bounce houses to rent out, drawing inspiration from her uncle's jumper business.

La Familia Jumpers stands out for its exceptional service and community care. Noemi's personal experiences as a Latina, a daughter of a single mother, and a former single parent herself, have shaped the ethos of her business. The company thrives on treating every event as their own, with love and unmatched dedication. Noemi's journey hasn't been without challenges, but her faith, the support of her family, and her unwavering commitment have been her guiding lights.

Noemi is deeply grateful to God, her family, friends, mentors, and especially her husband, who has been her pillar of strength. She hopes her story inspires others to chase their dreams, no matter the obstacles.
